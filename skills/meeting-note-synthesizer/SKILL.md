# Meeting Note Synthesizer

Use this skill when a meeting happened, but the team still needs a usable summary.

## Use this skill when

- transcript or notes are long and messy
- decisions and owners are buried in discussion
- someone needs a quick recap and next actions

## Required outputs

- executive summary
- decisions made
- action items with owners
- blockers and follow-ups

## Workflow

1. Extract the real decisions first.
2. Separate discussion from outcomes.
3. Capture named or implied owners when possible.
4. Turn vague follow-ups into explicit action items.
5. End with a short summary that can be forwarded directly.

## Guardrails

- Do not invent owners when none exist; mark as unassigned.
- Keep summaries brief and concrete.
- Favor actionability over transcript fidelity.
